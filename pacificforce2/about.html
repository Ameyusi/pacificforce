OUR STORY

Founded in 2018, Pacific Force has quickly risen to become one of the most recognized names in esports. What started as a passionate group of gamers with a shared vision has evolved into a professional organization competing at the highest levels across multiple titles.

Our journey began with a single PUBG team that defied expectations and claimed victory at several regional tournaments. Building on this success, we expanded into League of Legends and Valorant, assembling rosters of exceptional talent and dedication.

Today, Pacific Force stands as a symbol of excellence in the esports community, with a growing fanbase and a commitment to pushing the boundaries of competitive gaming.
