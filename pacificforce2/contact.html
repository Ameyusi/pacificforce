FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How can I join Pacific Force as a player?

We're always on the lookout for exceptional talent. To be considered for our roster, please send your gaming resume, achievements, and social media links to join@pacificforce.gg. Our talent scouts regularly review applications and will reach out if there's a potential fit.

How can my brand sponsor Pacific Force?

We offer various partnership opportunities for brands looking to connect with the esports community. Please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@pacificforce.gg with details about your company and sponsorship goals.

Do you offer merchandise for fans?

Yes! We have a range of official Pacific Force merchandise available in our online shop. From jerseys to accessories, you can represent Pacific Force in style.

How can I stay updated on Pacific Force news and matches?

The best way to stay updated is to follow us on social media and subscribe to our newsletter. You can also check our News and Schedule pages regularly for the latest updates.

Does Pacific Force offer internships or job opportunities?

Yes, we occasionally have openings for various roles within our organization. Check our social media channels for announcements or send your resume to careers@pacificforce.gg to be considered for future opportunities.
